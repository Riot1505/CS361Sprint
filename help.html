Welcome to the help page!

Here are a few steps to help with using the app.
The home page will show the block format of your goals. You can add goals in this page by
selecting the "Add Goal" button. That will give a small display, requiring the goal's name, description, priority, and reminder time, and deadline.
The priority section will visually show the Goals listed in terms of their priority.
The Reminder section will show each Goal. The calendar section will show the deadline of your goal.
Undo: The undo button will undo the last action that was made. If you deleted a goal, you can undo it within
the first session. Redo: The redo button will revert an undo. Note: The add goal webpage includes several required inputs. It may take time to fulfill each input.
